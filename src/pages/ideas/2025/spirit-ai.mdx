### Project Type: Large

## Description:

**Spirit AI** is an AI-powered mental health platform designed to provide **personalized emotional support, therapy conversations, mood tracking, guided meditations, and self-improvement tools**. This open-source project acts as a **digital therapist**, offering scientifically backed mental wellness guidance while integrating with **professional therapists when needed**.

## Expected Outcomes:

- **AI Therapy Chatbot (Core Feature)**  
  - An AI-driven conversational agent that offers emotional support based on cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT) and mindfulness techniques.

- **Mood & Emotion Tracking**  
  - Helps users log their moods and emotions over time, offering insights into patterns and potential mental health triggers.

- **Personalized Mental Health Programs**  
  - Provides AI-driven self-improvement plans tailored to the user's emotional state and long-term wellness goals.

- **Human Therapist Integration**  
  - Seamlessly connects users with professional therapists when advanced support is required.

- **Crisis Intervention & SOS Mode**  
  - Implements emergency response mechanisms, providing immediate support and directing users to crisis hotlines if needed.

- **AI-Powered Sleep & Relaxation Assistant**  
  - Generates guided meditations, sleep sounds, and breathing exercises to promote relaxation and improve sleep quality.
